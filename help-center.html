Welcome to the Help Center

Find answers to your questions and get support for Expense Tracker.

Account & Profile

Manage your login, profile, and account settings efficiently.

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Enter your name, email, verify it, enter password, then confirm your password and submit. You’ll be redirected to the login page once registration is complete. after logging in you'll be redirected to the dashboard.
First, check your spam/junk folder. If the email isn’t there, click on “Resend Verification Email” at the login screen. If the problem persists, please contact our support team.
Click on "Forgot Password" while logging in. Enter your registered email, and you’ll receive a reset password link. Follow the instructions to set a new password.
Go to your "Dashboard", click on "Settings" in the sidebar, update your information, and click "Save Settings". Your profile will be updated instantly.

Expenses

Track, categorize, and manage your expenses.

Click "Add Expense" in the sidebar. Fill in the details such as category, amount, and date, then click "Save". The expense will appear in your dashboard immediately.
Currently, expenses are read-only. Editing and deleting will be available in upcoming updates.
This happens when you add expenses without adding income. Make sure to add both income and expenses for accurate balance tracking. See the User Guide for more details.
While adding an expense, select the appropriate Category (e.g., Food, Travel, Shopping). Categorizing helps generate more accurate reports.

Reports

Analyze your financial insights and track spending trends.

Monthly reports are available on your dashboard under the "Reports" section. You can view charts and graphs of your income and expenses.
Yes, go to the "Reports" tab and use the category filter to view how much you’ve spent on Food, Travel, Shopping, and other categories.
Currently, exporting reports is not supported. This feature will be added in a future update.

Security

Keep your account safe with best practices.

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen to reset your password securely.
Immediately reset your password and contact support through the Contact Us page.
Always use a strong password, never share your login details, and log out when using public devices.

Settings

Customize your Expense Tracker experience.

Navigate to the "Settings" tab in your dashboard sidebar. You can update account details like name, email, or profile picture from there.
Currently, theme switching is not available. A dark mode option will be added in the future.
Please contact support if you wish to deactivate or permanently delete your account. This option will be provided in the settings soon.
