User Guide

Learn how to navigate and use the Expense Tracker effectively.

1. Getting Started

  • Sign up using your email and create a secure password.
  • Log in to access your personal dashboard.
  • Your account is secured with JWT authentication and refresh tokens.

2. Adding Expenses

  1. Go to your Dashboard.
  2. Click the "Add Expense" in the side bar.
  3. Fill in the details: amount, date, category, and description.
  4. Click Add Expense button to record your expense.

8. Smart Categorization

  • Create clear and meaningful categories for your expenses, such as Food, Travel, Shopping, Bills, etc.
  • Select catergory "Income" to add amount to you income section. Otherwise Your expenses will cause you a negative "Total balance"
  • Use consistent naming for categories to easily track spending trends.
  • Avoid too many categories; group small or irregular expenses under “Miscellaneous” to keep reporting simple.
  • Regularly review your categories and adjust them to match your spending habits for better insights.

3. Viewing Reports

  • Navigate to the Reports section.
  • View your expenses by category or by month.
  • Analyze your spending habits through charts and graphs.

4. Filtering Expenses

  • Use the Filter option to search expenses by date, category, or amount.
  • This helps you quickly find specific transactions and monitor spending trends.

5. Managing Your Profile

  • Access the Profile section from your dashboard.
  • Update your name, email, and password as needed.
  • Ensure your email is valid to receive notifications and password resets.

6. Security Tips

  • Never share your password with anyone.
  • Log out after using the Expense Tracker on public devices.
  • Regularly update your password for better security.

7. Support & Feedback

  • If you encounter issues, use the Contact Us section on the homepage to send your queries.
  • We aim to respond to all queries promptly.
